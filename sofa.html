Sofa Slackers

Sofa Slackers is a Unity 2D game made during the 2015 Global Game Jam. All work was done within 48 hours. It is a cooperative 2-player game where the goal is to catch falling "ideas" and NOT match 3 in a row. It is played with the keyboard (W and D for player 1 and left and right arrows for player 2). You can find the Global Game Jam page about it here.

The inspiration was from the theme: What Do We Do Now? Our team chose to interpret this as referring to that familiar sense of apathy and indecision when you can't decide on an activity.
