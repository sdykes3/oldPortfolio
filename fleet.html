Fleet Destroyer

This is my final project for a class about programming in C, which I took in Fall 2014. The premise is that you are hunting down and destroying enemy ships in the midst of an asteroid field. You must dodge or shoot down debris, collect healthpacks, and battle with enemy ships. There is also a cheat mode for invincibility.

Roles:
  • Idea and design
  • Coding and implementation

Development:
