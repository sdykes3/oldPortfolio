Argh! I'm Drowning!

Argh! I'm Drowning! is an Atari 2600 game created using Batari Basic. It is a single player game in which the goal is to survive as long as possible. The game can be played with an Atari 2600 emulator, such as Stella.

The premise of the game is that you are trapped in a tank of water and must swim the the surface to catch your breath. However, there are falling blocks at the surface which makes this risky. Being hit by one will knock the player back to the bottom of the tank. Additionally, there is a shark midway to the surface to make it even more challenging.

A large part of the game design is the idea that the player should get a feel for the struggle and difficulty of fighting off drowning. Therefore, the controls for ascending involve repeatedly tapping the up button, as if having to kick and struggle to ascend. While not kicking, the player will gradually sink to the bottom of the tank.
