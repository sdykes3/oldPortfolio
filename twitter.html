True Love Bot

October 2013

True Love Bot is a project I made while experimenting with APIs. It is a bot that automatically posts to Twitter every hour or so, tweeting about its realtionship status with semi-random text samples.

True Love Bot will cycle through "stages" of relationships, from new love, to hitting problems in the relationship, then to being in denial about enjoying a single lifestyle. After a while it does it all over again with a differently named lover.
