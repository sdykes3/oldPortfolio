T-Square

This a is mobile app design for Georgia Tech's course management site. T-Square is used by students to view and submit homework assignments, check grades, see recent announcements, find lecture materials, and more.

My design process for this project began with writing out design goals and what I would like to improve from the regular T-Square site. Next, I sketched various layout ideas and made note of ideal interactions where applicable. I conducted a survey about common usage of T-Square in order to decide on general layout and hierarchy of some elements. From there, I created low fidelity wireframes and a map using Balsamiq, and then high fidelity whiteframes in Illustrator. The final colored mockups were also created using Illustrator.

This app is based heavily on principles of material design, as it includes common components such as cards and "paper" sheets upon which elements are placed. The color palette and typography are also drawn from material design.
